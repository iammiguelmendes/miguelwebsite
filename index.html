About Project 3

I improvised and asked a friend of mine what he thought about websites of gaming computer stores and tech stores, and pretented in my mind that he was my client/company product.

Defined problem: Traditional design and repetitive results (normally in these stores, if you search a computer or a smartphone, the catalog shows your product several times, one time for each available color, for example if you search "Iphone 8", the catalog shows three Iphones 8, "Iphone 8 - WHITE", "Iphone 8 - GREY", "Iphone 8 - BLACK", which my friend disliked).

Decided to not use a traditional design and to add an option to choose the color, in the product's page.

Created the prototype, which shall be continued, just like the others.
